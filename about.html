Who We Are

Gervin Tires is a family owned business offering name brand new and used tires, a wide selection of custom wheels, and full automotive repair services — all under one roof in Brockton, Massachusetts. We've been a trusted part of the South Shore community for over ten years, and every customer who walks through our doors becomes part of the Gervin family.

Our shop is located at 1181 Main Street, Brockton, MA 02301. Stop by, give us a call for a quote, or schedule your appointment online — we're here to help.

Our Goal

Our goal is simple: focus on customer service. We employ a well-trained staff specializing in the sale and installation of passenger, performance, light truck, and SUV tires. Whether you're looking for the best deal on a budget-friendly set or a premium performance upgrade, we'll point you in the right direction — honestly, without pressure.

We carry name brand tires from the most trusted manufacturers in the industry and back every sale with expert installation and alignment services. When you buy tires at Gervin, you're not just buying rubber — you're buying the confidence that comes with knowing the job was done right.

What We Offer

From a simple oil change to a full suspension overhaul, our technicians handle it all. We're a one-stop shop for drivers in Brockton and the surrounding South Shore communities.
